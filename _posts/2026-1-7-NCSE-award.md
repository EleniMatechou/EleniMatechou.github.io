My PhD student, Cosmo De-Bonis Campbell, won "Most commended presentation" award at the 2025 NCSE meeting in St Andrews.

![Cosmo-award](/images/Media (3).jfif)

Cosmo's PhD is part-funded by Butterfly Conservation and aims to develop fast and flexible methods for their data on butterflies and moths.

The talk presented Cosmo's work on spatio-temporal neural networks for citizen science data.
